         (i) The Company requires services of Custom Clearance.

         a) The Service Provider shall be responsible to co-ordinate with office of the Company and               the other offices for performance of all services.The Company and Service Provider               agree to appoint their respective SPOC.          d) The Service Provider shall liaise with port Authorities, Custom Officials for effective &               smooth clearance of shipmentthrough all ports in India.

Unless specifically               required by the Company in writing, the Service Provider shall not handle the               transportation of the cargo from the port/warehouse as the case may beand shall not be               held liable in any manner.
         f) The Service Provider will be available to provide the services on 24/7* 365 days basis as               may be required by the Company.
         g) The Service Provider shall be responsible for loading of material at the port by providing               required labour assistance onchargeable basis.
         h) Service Provider is responsible for ensuring the delivery of the consignment as per the               packing list and handing over thesame to the Transporter with proper acknowledgement.
         i) Service Provider shall liaise, co –ordinate and ensure that the cargo is loaded onto the               trailers / truck properly and such loading conforms to any standard loading and               transporting procedure / methodology applicable and followed by anyindustry for such               similar applications.
         j) The Service Provider shall not be obligated to provide any additional services unless               specifically requested in writing bythe Company post mutually agreeing to the service               charges involved.

         ¤ Copy of packing list
